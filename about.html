About This Project

Why "Don't Click Too Soon"?

We've all been there: clicking "I Agree" without reading the terms, or submitting forms without double-checking. This site exists to slow you down (in a good way) and give you tools to understand what you're agreeing to or filing.

Our Philosophy

  • Privacy First: All tools run in your browser. We collect no data.
  • Open Source: Our code is transparent and verifiable.
  • Free Forever: Bureaucracy is hard enough without paywalls.
  • User-Friendly: Complex processes, simplified.

Current Tools

Tax Form Filler (2025): Automatically fill IRS Forms 1040 and 8949 from Excel spreadsheets. Perfect for tracking capital gains from stock sales, crypto, or ESPP transactions.

Coming Soon

Terms Simplified: Paste any Terms & Conditions and get a plain-English summary of what you're actually agreeing to.

Technical Details

Built with TypeScript, Vite, and pdf-lib. All processing happens client-side using modern web APIs. No servers, no databases, no tracking.
